Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Emanta, the grade point average (GPA) calculator application, collects, uses, and discloses your information. Emanta is designed to be privacy-focused. We do not collect any personal identifiable information (PII) from you. This means we do not collect information such as your name, email address, student ID, or any data that could be used to identify you as an individual. The only information you enter into Emanta is the data related to your GPA calculation, such as: Course names (optional) Grades (e.g., A, B, C) Credit hours (optional) This data is processed solely on your device to perform the GPA calculation. It is not stored by Emanta or transmitted anywhere else. Emanta is intended for general audiences and does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you believe your child has provided us with information, please contact us. We will take steps to remove such information from our records.
